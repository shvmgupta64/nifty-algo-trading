This is the data of backtesting.
Trade rules are as below:

Trade should be on NIFTY.
Indicator will be used 15-21 EMA.
When Nifty is in long trend on 5 mins timeframe:
	Without Slope (21 EMA angle doesn't matter).
	15 EMA Above 21 EMA
	Bullish Rejection Candle which is touching any one of the ema.
	Candle body should be atleast 10 points, or if lower wick is greater than 15 points and high is less than 4 points above from close.
	NIFTY will be bought with the SL of Candle low and target will be 1:2 Risk Reward.

When Nifty is in long trend on 5 mins timeframe:
	With Slope (21 EMA angle should be greater than 30 to avoid sideways).
	15 EMA Above 21 EMA
	Bullish Rejection Candle which is touching any one of the ema.
	Candle body should be atleast 10 points, or if lower wick is greater than 15 points and high is less than 4 points above from close.
	NIFTY will be bought with the SL of Candle low and target will be 1:2 Risk Reward.

Will be reverse for short trade.
